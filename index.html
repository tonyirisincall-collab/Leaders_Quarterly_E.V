DO Achievement
Actual DOs completed by the team vs the target for the period. Pulled directly from Dialer / EyeBeam call logs exported to Google Sheets.
Data source: At end of the evaluation period, export call results from Dialer and EyeBeam to your Google Sheet. Count completed DOs per agent. Sum the team total. Compare to the target set for that period.
DO Achievement — Continuous Linear Scoring
Dialer + EyeBeam export → Google Sheets
Auto from Sheets
# Pull team total from Dialer + EyeBeam logs
DO_Rate = ( Team_Actual_DOs ÷ Team_Target_DOs ) × 100

# Score is continuous — no bands, no thresholds
DO_Score = ( DO_Rate ÷ 100 ) × 80  # max 80 pts

# Example: achieved 50 out of 100 target DOs → DO_Rate = 50% → DO_Score = 40 / 80
How it works: The TL's DO achievement percentage maps directly to their DO score. 100% achievement = full 80 points. 50% achievement = 40 points. 0% = 0 points. No steps, no bands — every DO counts.
Zero DOs: Any period with zero team DOs triggers automatic review regardless of reason. Zero target = score is 0 until a target is set.

Rating Bands & Formula
Full Scoring Formula
# DO: continuous linear — every % point counts
DO_score         = (do_actual / do_target) × 80  # capped at 80 if over 100%

# Remaining: 0–4 band scores, normalised to their weight
coaching_score   = (coaching_pts / 4) × 7
biz_score        = avg(reports_pts, subtasks_pts, followups_pts) / 4 × 5
leadership_score = avg(escalations_pts, punctuality_pts) / 4 × 4
motivation_score = avg(attendance_pts, complaints_pts, transfers_pts) / 4 × 4

────────────────────────────────────
TOTAL = sum of all above  # max = 100 pts
