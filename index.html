DO Achievement
Actual DOs completed by the team vs the target for the period. Pulled directly from Dialer / EyeBeam call logs exported to Google Sheets.
Data source: At end of the evaluation period, export call results from Dialer and EyeBeam to your Google Sheet. Count completed DOs per agent. Sum the team total. Compare to the target set for that period.

Rating Bands & Formula
Full Scoring Formula
# Each metric scored 0–4, normalised to its weight

DO_score         = (do_pts / 4) × 80
coaching_score   = (coaching_pts / 4) × 7
biz_score        = avg(reports_pts, subtasks_pts, followups_pts) / 4 × 5
leadership_score = avg(escalations_pts, punctuality_pts) / 4 × 4
motivation_score = avg(absent_pts, complaints_pts, transfers_pts) / 4 × 4

────────────────────────────────────
TOTAL = sum of all above  # max = 100 pts
